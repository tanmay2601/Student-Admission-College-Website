INTRODUCTION

J.S.S. Academy of Technical Education, Noida (JSSATEN), in its full name Jagadguru Sri Shivarathreeshwara Academy of Technical Education, Noida is a private educational institute situated in Sector 62, Noida, Uttar Pradesh and affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Gautam Buddha Nagar.The institute's campus was inaugurated in 1998.
The Science and Technology Entrepreneurs Park is a registered society under the Society Registration Act, 1860.
